Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized learning experience, we collect the following information from our users:

  • Personal Details: Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Transaction Details: Payment details and UTR numbers for manual verification.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of enhancing your experience on Mock Matrix Hub:

  • Account Setup: To create and maintain your unique student profile.
  • Personal Analytics: To track your mock test progress and provide performance insights.
  • Exam Updates: To send important notifications regarding new tests, exam dates, and platform updates.

3. Zero Data Sharing Policy

We respect your privacy. Mock Matrix Hub strictly adheres to a No-Data-Selling policy. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third-party marketing agencies or external services.

4. Data Security

Your data is highly secured using advanced encryption protocols. We use industry-standard security measures to ensure that your information is protected from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your sensitive data is stored in our secure database managed through Supabase.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your preferences. These cookies do not store any personal information.
